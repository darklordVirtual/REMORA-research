REMORA is the name of this Apache-2.0 open-source research prototype. The word
"remora" is also widely used elsewhere and refers to a fish species, so this
policy does not try to reserve every use of the REMORA word.

AROMER is the protected brand identity associated with Luftfiber AS and Stian
Skogbrott. AROMER logos, product identity, service positioning, and confusingly
similar marks are reserved.

## Allowed Use

You may use the REMORA name to:

- refer truthfully to this open-source project,
- describe compatibility with the Apache-2.0 REMORA codebase,
- cite REMORA in academic or technical work,
- identify unmodified copies or forks when attribution is clear.

## Restricted Use

You may not use the AROMER name, AROMER logos, Luftfiber AS product identity, or
confusingly similar marks to:

- imply endorsement, certification, partnership, or official status without
  written permission,
- market a hosted, managed, SaaS, compliance, audit, or support service as
  "AROMER", "Official AROMER", or a Luftfiber AS service without a separate
  agreement,
- remove or obscure attribution to Luftfiber AS and Stian Skogbrott,
- present modified versions as the official AROMER product.

## Forks and Commercial Products

Forks and commercial products built on the Apache-2.0 code should use distinct
product names and state their relationship accurately, for example:

> Built with the Apache-2.0 REMORA open-source governance engine.

They should not imply that Stian Skogbrott operates, certifies, supports, or
endorses the derived product unless a separate written agreement says so.
